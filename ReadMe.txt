# Academic-List
Assistance to students in making of a list of references.
There are two options for dynamic output:
    1) in php -> available here https://saliens.online
    2) at the vue.js
In the drop-down list, select the source of literature -> fill in the form fields -> click "Done", 
and then vue.js will collect info from form fields.
